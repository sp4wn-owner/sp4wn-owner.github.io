Sp4wn App



In the mobile app, users can connect their robot to the platform allowing others to control it over the internet. For example, you connect your drone to our app and someone accross the world can fly it; however latency from that distance may be undesirable. Another example might be setting up a robot in your retail store to give shoppers a remote yet in-person experience. Think TikTok, but with telepresence robots.

Telepresence Robots



Outside of the app, we will design our own line of telepresence robots. The first few versions will pair the actuators (motors) of the robot with a smartphone over Bluetooth. Later versions might include their own cameras and sensors. Developers/hobbyists will be able to purchase keys to connect their own robots to our platform.

Monetization



Hosts charge for access to their robot(s) by setting their own price/min. We charge hosts a 20% platform fee (subject to change).
